Bio

I am a postdoctoral researcher with department of computer science, University of Maryland, College park, advised by Professor Matthias Zwicker. I obtained my B.E. (2009), M.E. (2012) and Ph.D (2012) from Northwestern Polytechnical University in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems. My research interests include 3D computer vision, digital geometry processing, and artifical intelligence.
